"Preset_Notes=Mild Dyslexia in Children
by Ngawang Samten

This preset is for Dyslexia in children. It is to be used for specific cases in which there is a very pronounced difficulty in reading and comprehension (even when a text is read to the child by an adult), but not to the point of jumping/reverse/mirror letters or words. This is intended primarily for a new minority of children with very non-conceptual style of thinking, who are extremely sensitive and emphatic and have a style of perception mainly based on visual images and feelings. These kids are very good at grasping complex topics at a glance, and at learning with their hands, but have difficulty processing linear information. This set will help with that deficiency without re-introducing the faults which come with an overly conceptual mindset. 

It is intended for remote and to be used with a sine wave (damped or normal, depending on the specific case - experiment). The voltage should be set between 10V and 20V (again, it is person specific). The set can be left to run on remote for a couple of days for initial evaluation. If your child is particularly sensitive, turn it off at night as it might keep them from falling asleep.  "
