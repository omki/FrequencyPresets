Method:
----------
Rife's frequencies are multiplied so as to give a harmonic close to 3.1Mhz. 
The lowest and highest harmonics are then used as a guide for the low & high settings of a spectrum sweep.

Spectrum sweep is designed differently here in that only 10 child frequencies are used; this is so power retained is greater and our 'comb' does not try to do too much at once to the DNA.

According to Spooky2 interface, frequencies are applied for at least 1.5 hrs.
A low 0.6 Hz gate is applied to create a pumping effect.

Transmits two simultaneous sweeps - one proceeds from low to high, the other from high to low.

Since the application is intense the sweep only runs once.
