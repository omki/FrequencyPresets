This is my latest improvement in dual weight spectrum sweeping. You can further improve results by running parts 1 - 4 simultaneously using 4 generators.

The dual weighted sweep is using the main sweep frequency divided by 4. Consequently, its span is also 1/4th that of the main sweep and therefore, it will cover only one fourth of the main sweep span. 

When you select a dual weighted sweep, spooky calculates the offset so that the first quarter of the sweep is dual weighted. But you could as well choose one of the three other quarters. Below are provided the formulas enabling you to calculate the various offsets :

F1 and F2 are the beginning and the ending frequencies of the sweep as given by Spooky, the offsets adjusted to dual weight the four quarters (Q1 to Q4) are given as:

Q1 : OUT2 = 1/4 * OUT1 + 3/4 * F1                     (this one is provided by Spooky when you select a dual weighted sweep)
Q2 : OUT2 = 1/4 * OUT1 + 1/2 * F1 + 1/4 * F2
Q3 : OUT2 = 1/4 * OUT1 + 1/4 * F1 + 1/2 * F2
Q4 : OUT2 = 1/4 * OUT1 + 3/4 * F2

If you prefer to center the dual sweep in the middle of the main sweep, the offset is :

OUT2 = 1/4 * OUT1 + 3/8 * (F1+F2)
